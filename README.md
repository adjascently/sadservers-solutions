##  About SadServers

SadServers Inc. is a Canada-based company (Waterloo, Ontario) that provides realistic technical challenges for learning and evaluating engineering skills.

Since its launch in 2022, SadServers has:
- Helped tens of thousands of users  
- Powered 500,000+ Linux & DevOps challenges  
- Been used by companies to train teams and assess candidates  
- Supported organizations like Metr, the AI Security Institute, and SRE-focused AI companies  

Backed by the **Accelerator Centre**, SadServers is built on a simple philosophy:

> **Do. Learn. Assess.**
